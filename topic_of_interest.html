1. Introduction

Tomato and scrambled eggs is a staple dish in Chinese home cooking, valued for its balance of sweet and savory flavors, soft texture, and simple preparation. The acidity of ripe tomatoes blends with the richness of scrambled eggs to create a comforting, nutritious meal that is fast to prepare and widely loved.

2. Cooking Steps

  1. Beat 3 eggs with a pinch of salt; cut 2 tomatoes into wedges.
  2. Heat oil, scramble eggs until half-cooked, remove from the pan.
  3. Add tomatoes to the pan, stir-fry until soft and juicy; add salt and optional sugar.
  4. Return eggs to the pan, stir-fry to combine evenly.
  5. Serve immediately!

3. Tips

  • Use ripe tomatoes for better flavor and more juice.
  • Avoid overcooking the eggs to retain softness.
  • A small amount of sugar can help balance the tomato's acidity.
  • Add sesame oil at the end for extra aroma if desired.
